# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `docs-drift` to be useful to maintainers across ecosystems. Participation should be respectful, direct, and technically constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus feedback on the code, behavior, or proposal
- Share reproduction steps for false positives and false negatives
- Assume good faith, but be precise about breakage and risk
- Keep discussion accessible to contributors with different experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or intimidation
- Deliberate bad-faith bug reports or spam
- Sharing private credentials, secrets, or sensitive repository data in issues

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that harms collaboration. Repeated abuse may lead to loss of participation rights in the project spaces.
